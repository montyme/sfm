What is Unauthorized SFMOMA Show

Unauthorized SFMOMA Show is a series of solo shows that take place within SFMOMA’s public spaces from April 6th - July 2nd on a rotating basis. Similar to SFMOMA’s mission statement, Unauthorized SFMOMA Show is dedicated to making the art for our time a vital and meaningful part of public life. Unauthorized SFMOMA Show is also guided by our commitment to fostering creativity and embracing new ways of seeing the world and the institutions that aim to represent it. To fulfill this goal, we have created a space where to exhibit and share the artwork that everybody wants to see exhibited and shared.

How to exhibit work

In order to participate in the exhibition go to "I WANT TO HAVE AN UNAUTHORIZED SFMOMA SHOW", fill in the form, upload your work in digital format (.jpg, .png or .gif) and press "SUBMIT". All works are immediately accepted, each submission automatically constituting an Unauthorized SFMOMA Solo Show on view at SFMOMA via the same website ( http://sfmoma.show ). Each exhibition starts when a work is submitted and ends when a new work is submitted. There are NO application or exhibition fees.

How to visit the exhibition

Unauthorized SFMOMA Shows take place at SFMOMA’s public spaces via http://sfmoma.show and thus, the only requirement to visit Unauthorized SFMOMA Shows is to be physically present at SFMOMA and have access to a device that can connect to the Internet. Once there, go to http://sfmoma.show to see the show (please, allow location access to Unauthorized SFMOMA Show in order to confirm your location). Note that public spaces at SFMOMA don't require ticket and thus Unauthorized SFMOMA Shows are free to visit.

Catalog

A catalog featuring all Unauthorized SFMOMA Shows will be printed in summer 2017.
